INTRODUCING THE NEW

EDUZE APP

Eduze is a transportation and technology start-up established to alleviate the transportation and mobility problem many locals of Mahikeng and the whole of North West experience in getting to and from town. Arriving at school or work on time or heading back home after a strenuous day shouldn't be a pain. Take Eduze!

Our purpose is to make life easier for the people we serve. We have an overaching goal of continuous improvement and expansion. We believe that technology coupled with an understanding of our customers needs plays a big role in achieving that objective.
